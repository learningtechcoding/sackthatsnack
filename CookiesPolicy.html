Cookies Policy

At Sack That Snack, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, and analyze our website traffic. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. These files help us remember your preferences, track site usage, and improve our services, ensuring you have the tastiest experience possible.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to remember items in your cart, save your favorite snack choices, and offer personalized recommendations. They also help us track performance so we can keep improving our menu and website.

Types of Cookies We Use

- **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for basic site functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.
- **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
- **Marketing Cookies**: Used to display personalized snack promotions and ads.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect your ability to place orders or use some features of Sack That Snack effectively.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services like Google Analytics or social media plugins to use cookies on our site to help us enhance your experience and reach more snack lovers.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here so you always know what data we collect and how we use it.

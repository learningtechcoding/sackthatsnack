Privacy Policy

At Sack That Snack, your privacy is a top priority. We’re committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring your snack experience is both safe and satisfying.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, delivery address, phone number, and payment information. We also gather data about how you use our site through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process orders, provide customer support, personalize your experience, send promotional offers (with your consent), and improve our website. We never sell your information to third parties.

Sharing Your Data

We only share your data with trusted partners like delivery services or payment processors as needed to fulfill your orders. All partners follow strict data protection standards.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and stay alert.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party services (like social media or delivery apps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites, so please review their policies separately.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. Please review it regularly to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out via our contact form or email. We’re always happy to help!
